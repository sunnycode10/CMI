About Us

Sharing Gospel Hope with Those in Need Around the World

CMI OUTREACH MINISTRIES

Faith-based Organisation

Welcome to Christ Mandate International Outreach Ministries, a dynamic Christian evangelical outreach fueled by our passionate commitment to follow wherever Jesus leads us.

Our calling is clear: to spread the transformative message of the gospel and extend a compassionate hand to those in need. At the heart of our mission is a deep desire to empower individuals and entire communities for the glory of Jesus Christ.

Feature Programs

CMI programs Includes:

SPREADING THE GOSPEL
Transforming Lives Through the Power of Christ

Central to our mission is the proclamation of the gospel—the good news that Jesus Christ, the Son of God, offers salvation, grace, and eternal life to all who believe. We understand that true empowerment begins with a personal encounter with the love of Christ. Through evangelistic initiatives, community outreach, and intentional relationships, we strive to create spaces where individuals can experience the transformative power of the Holy Spirit and God's Word.

Read More
HOLISTIC MINISTRY
Empowering Lives, Embodying Christ's Compassion

Our commitment to empowerment goes beyond words; it extends to action. Recognizing that physical and material needs often accompany spiritual ones, we engage in holistic ministry. This involves reaching out to those facing hunger, providing medical assistance to the sick, and offering financial support to those in need. By addressing both the spiritual and practical aspects of people’s lives, we seek to embody the love and compassion of Jesus Christ in tangible ways.

Social Welfare Programs

Our social welfare programs are dedicated to meeting the practical needs of communities. Through initiatives such as food distribution, medical aid, and educational support, we extend the love and compassion of Christ to those facing challenges and hardships.

Personal Development programs
