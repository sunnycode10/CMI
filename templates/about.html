Our Journey

Our journey is not defined by geographical boundaries but by the divine call to go wherever Jesus sends us. Inspired by the “Great commission (Matthew 28:1920), we embark on a mission that transcends cultural, linguistic, and Societal barriers.

Alexander Obi, Founder

Our vision is expansive, reaching every corner of the world with the life-changing message of God’s love. We believe that the gospel is not confined to specific place but is a universal truth that has the power to transform lives and communities.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

In the pursuit of a profound mission rooted in the teachings of Christ, Our Christian evangelism outreach ministry envisions a world where the transformative power of God’s love reigns supreme. We are driven by the unwavering belief that through international and compassionate evangelism, hearts can be ignited with the flame of faith, fostering a vibrant and inclusive community bound together by the common thread of divine grace.

Mission & Values

At CMI Outreach Ministries, Our mission is to promote the gospel of Jesus Christ and extend a helping hand to those in need. Rooted in faith and compassion, our values guide us in serving communities, fostering personal development, and spreading the message of love, hope, and redemption.
